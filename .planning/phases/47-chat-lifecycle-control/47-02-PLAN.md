## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-47-archive-auth | Elevation of Privilege | archiveConversationAction | mitigate | `requireAdmin()` called at action entry — rejects non-admin sessions before any DB access |

| T-47-tx-race | Tampering (Data Integrity) | createNewConversation $transaction | accept | Two simultaneous tabs create two new conversations — both old convs deactivated; user ends up with two new empty conversations, no data lost; acceptable per RESEARCH.md Pitfall 4 |
